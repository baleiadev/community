# Baleia.dev Community 🐳

Welcome to the official GitHub repository for the **Baleia.dev Community**. 

We are a community-driven educational initiative based in Brazil, focused on removing barriers to entry for Cloud Native technologies. Our mission is to provide hands-on, high-quality, and free education on **Continuous Delivery, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, GitOps, and Platform Engineering**.

🌐 **Official Website:** [baleia.dev](https://baleia.dev)
💬 **Discord Community:** [Join us here](https://discord.com/invite/uscjRgpr3A)

## 🎯 Our Mission
Our goal is to build a welcoming environment where beginners and students can confidently start their Cloud Native journey, while professionals can deepen their engineering skills. We believe in learning by doing: every meetup is paired with open-source labs and technical materials hosted right here.

## 👨‍🏫 Leadership & Governance
The community is organized and led by a **Docker Captain**, bringing together academic methodology and real-world enterprise experience in building scalable Cloud architectures.
